About

Mrs Rugmini Ganesh is a dedicated French Tutor with over seventeen years of experience in this line. She develops the students to attain competency in the French language as per their grade level; teaches various tiers, inculcates motivational and societal skills in students, assists students to get a hang of the subject, monitors students during examination and conducts counselling sessions for students.

After completing twenty years of successful inning in the banking profession, her passion for teaching led her to acquire many professional qualifications in the education field. To name a few are Early Childhood Care and Education; Post-Graduate Diploma in Psychological Counselling; and multiple levels of French qualifications like DELF A1, A2, B1 etc. She also has to her credit Cours Pour Professeurs, Niveau B1-B2 in French.
