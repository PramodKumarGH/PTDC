About

Shri G Venkatesan is an enthusiastic trainer aligned with 36 years of field experience in the development of human resources in premier R&D Institutions of the Government of India. The last position he held was Director (ATI) in the Department of Atomic Energy (DAE), Mumbai. He also held the responsibilities of Nodal Grievance Officer and Liaison Officer for SC/ST in the DAE.

He is a Law Graduate from Mumbai University and holds Post-Graduation in Public Policy Management from MDI Gurgaon and George Mason University, Virginia, USA. He also holds a Diploma in Training and Development from IISTD, recognized by Government of India. He has rich experience in conducting sessions on Discipline and Vigilance, Constitution, Reservation in Service, Train the Trainer, Stress Management, Yoga and so on. He is a qualified Yoga teacher, Yoga Therapist and member of Indian Yoga Association.
