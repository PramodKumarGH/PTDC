Workshop on Customer Care

For Whom?

Professionals in-charge of Customer Service.

Organiser and Certificate

Organised by Professional Training and Development Centre, Bengaluru.

Contents:

  • Customer Service – Definition
  • Types of Guests
  • Basic Etiquettes
  • Role in Enhancing Business
  • Factors Supporting Personality Enhancement

Methodology

The program will be conducted through a highly participative method. The course will be delivered by expert faculty members using a variety of media. Participants will receive relevant and useful books/training materials.

Duration and Venue

Duration: 3 hours - online

Batch Size: Maximum 16 participants

Program Fees

Rs. 500/- per participant

Benefits

  • Exceptional customer service creates a memorable experience for guests, helping to build business success.
  • Participants will learn to support customer-dealing roles effectively.

Objectives

  • Define Customer Care.
  • Identify different types of guests and the care required when dealing with them.
  • Explain and demonstrate basic etiquettes in various situations.
  • Understand the do’s and don’ts for overall ambience management.
  • Enhance personality through key behavioral strategies.
