Workshop on Front Office/Corporate Reception

For Whom?

New entrants or professionals in front-facing roles in the corporate sector, hospitality, or tourism industry.

Organiser and Certificate

Organised by Professional Training and Development Centre, Bengaluru.

Contents:

  • Working effectively as part of the Front Office team
  • Making a positive first impression
  • Presentation skills and body language
  • Business etiquette and corporate grooming
  • Front of house reception operations
  • Communication skills and their importance
  • Handling arrivals, departures, and appointments
  • Booking and reservation management
  • Telephone skills
  • Preparing customer accounts and handling departures
  • Providing information and concierge services
  • Creating and filing documents in a business environment
  • Data protection
  • Managing customer complaints and compliance
  • Promoting additional services and upselling
  • Effective handover
  • Handling and recording payments, float, and petty cash

Methodology

The program will be highly participative, delivered using various media by expert faculty with international exposure. Participants will receive useful reference links.

Duration and Venue

Duration: 6 hours - online

Batch Size: Maximum 16 participants

Benefits

  • Work effectively as part of the Front Office team.
  • Create a positive first impression.

  • Understand business etiquette and corporate grooming.
