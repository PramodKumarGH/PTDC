Introduction to Group Discussion

For Whom?

Students and Professionals

Organiser and Certificate

Professional Training and Development Centre, Bengaluru

Duration and Venue

Duration: One day

Venue: Bengaluru

Batch Size

Minimum 8 and Maximum 20

Program Fees

Rs. 1000/- per participant

Methodology

The program will be conducted through a highly participative method. The course will be delivered by expert faculty members using a variety of media. Participants will receive relevant and useful books/training materials.

Contents:

  • What is Group Discussion
  • How to prepare for GD
  • Body Language
  • Listening and active participation
  • Do’s and Don’ts

Benefits

Group Discussions help in evaluating personality in a team setting and have become integral in selection processes. This workshop aims to boost confidence and preparation for GDs.

Objectives

  • Define what is GD
  • Conduct a SWOT analysis
  • Describe the importance of body language
  • Explain active listening
  • List the do’s and don’ts in a GD
