Introduction to Writing Effectively

For Whom?

College Students, Engineers, Scientists, Project Managers, and other professionals involved in writing exams, preparing documents, or presenting technical proposals.

Organiser and Certificate

Professional Training and Development Centre, Bengaluru

Duration and Venue

Duration: Two days - online

Batch Size: Minimum 8 and Maximum 20

Programme Fees

Rs. 1500/- per participant

Methodology

The programme will be conducted using a highly participative method. The course will be delivered through various media by expert faculty members with international experience. Participants will receive relevant books and training materials.

Contents

  • Systematic Approach to Writing
  • Common Errors in English Usage
  • Basics of Technical Communication
  • Visual Aids
  • Structuring of Documents
  • Advanced MS Word/PowerPoint Tools

Benefits

This workshop aims to enhance writing skills for effective documentation and proposal creation, essential for professional success and communication.

Objectives

  • Describe a systematic approach to writing.
  • Identify common errors and explain the rules.
  • Explain the features of technical communication.
  • Understand the need for visual aids.
  • Describe document structure and characteristics.
  • Use tools to create impactful proposals.
