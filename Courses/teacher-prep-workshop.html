Teacher Preparatory Workshop (TPW)

For Whom?

All those interested in taking up teaching as a profession.

Organiser and Certificate

Professional Training and Development Centre, Bengaluru

Contents

  • Characteristics of a Good Teacher
  • Learning vs Teaching
  • Salient Features of Self-Instructional Materials
  • Setting Objectives
  • Design a Teaching Module on a Given Topic
  • Prepare a Lesson Plan

Methodology

The programme is conducted using a highly participative method with various media by expert faculty members. Participants will receive relevant books and training materials.

Duration and Venue

Duration: 9 hours - online

Batch Size: Maximum 20

Programme Fees

Rs. 1250/- per participant

Benefits

This workshop focuses on creating effective teaching experiences by covering essential teaching skills and methodologies.

Objectives

  • State the essential characteristics of a good teacher (30 minutes)
  • State the difference between learning and teaching (30 minutes)
  • List out the features of self-instructional materials (60 minutes)
  • Define SMART objectives (60 minutes)
  • Design and deliver a teaching module on a given topic (180 minutes)
  • Set SMART objectives, select contents, prepare a lesson plan, and develop assessment tools
