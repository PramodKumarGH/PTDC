The Power of Effective Communication

For Whom?

Students, executives, managers, or any professional in sales, marketing, teaching, training, or facilitating roles.

Organiser and Certificate

Organised by Professional Training and Development Centre, Bengaluru.

Contents:

  • Communication Skillset Overview
  • Globalisation and Communication Style Changes
  • Interpersonal and Group Communication
  • Verbal and Non-verbal Communication
  • Body Language
  • Active and Passive Listening
  • Preparing a Video Resume (Theory and Practice)

Methodology

The programme will be highly participative, delivered using various media by expert faculty with international exposure. Participants will receive useful books and training materials.

Duration and Venue

Duration: 6 hours online

Batch Size: Minimum 12, Maximum 20 participants

Programme Fees

Rs. 1000/- per participant

Benefits

Effective communication is essential for navigating global connections and cultural diversity. This programme sharpens the skills necessary to meet today's communication demands.

Objectives

  • Explain the importance of communication skills in a global business environment.
  • Identify the skillset required for effective communication.
  • Discuss communication do’s and don’ts across diverse cultures.
  • Develop mutual respect in communication.
  • Demonstrate persuasion and influencing skills.
  • Enhance listening skills.
  • Give and receive feedback effectively.
  • Prepare a professional video resume.
