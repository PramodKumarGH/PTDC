Instructional Design and User Manual Development Workshop

For Whom?

Students, engineers, managers, or any professionals involved in developing user manuals or instructional materials for teaching, training, and facilitating.

Organiser and Certificate

Professional Training and Development Centre, Bengaluru

Contents:

  • Fundamentals of Instructional Design/User Manual
  • Difference between Instructional Design and User Manual
  • Audience Analysis
  • Systematic Approach to Writing
  • The ADDIE Model
  • Communication Skillset Overview
  • Globalisation and Paradigm Shift in Communication Styles
  • Formatting Issues and Solutions

Methodology

The programme uses a highly participative method with various media by expert faculty members. Participants will receive relevant books and training materials.

Duration and Venue

Duration: 6 hours

Batch Size: Minimum 12 and Maximum 20

Programme Fees

Rs. 1000/- per participant

Benefits

This workshop addresses the need for effective user manuals and instructional materials in an increasingly digital world, enhancing skills in creating user-friendly content and self-instructional materials.

Objectives

  • Explain the key features of user manuals and instructional designs.
  • Describe a systematic approach to writing.
  • Explain audience analysis and its importance.
  • Use formatting tools for effective document preparation.
  • Prepare a one-to-two minute video demo.
